The $250 Cookies AKA Neiman Marcus Cookies

Ratings


I'm going to give a disclaimer with these and say that if you don't like oatmeal cookies or walnuts you will not enjoy these. They're also entirely a labor of love and I spilled oats all over my apartment while making these. I ate more of this dough than I did any previous cookie. I am also biased because these are the cookies my mom made for me growing up.
Texture: 10/10 (solid yet chewy in the center)
Flavor: 10/10 (the mix of the oatmeal and chocolate and walnut is too good)
Ease of recipe: 6/10 (these are the first cookies I didn't have to chill, howver they do involve grinding the oats in a food processor and toasting nuts in a pan which is not necessarily easy. Also hand grating a chocolate bar is hard and messy work and I grates of a chunk of my newly painted finger nails so that sucked.)
Overall: 9/10 (these are a time commitment but oh my god. They are so good.)
